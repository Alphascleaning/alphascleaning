About Us

My name is Alpha, and I founded Alphas Cleaning with one clear goal: to deliver reliable, high-quality cleaning services that clients can truly depend on.

With a background in warehouse and logistics management, I bring structure, discipline, and efficiency into every project. Whether it’s a private home, an office, or a commercial building, we treat every property with the same care and attention to detail.

We believe in clear communication, punctuality, and consistent results. When you book with us, you know exactly what to expect — no surprises, just clean spaces.
